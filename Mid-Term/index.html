People often associate calories as something bad.

What is a calorie?
A calorie is the calculated amount of energy gained from eating something.
Therefore
Calories are simply a way to measure how much food one needs to eat
Calories only tell a fraction of what your body needs
Macros also play a role in your bodily needs
Macros are:
PROTEIN
Protien is important for growing and maintaining muscle mass, as well as repairing tissue in the body.
CARBOHYDRATES
Carbohydrates are essential nutrients that your body turns into glucose, giving you the energy to function.
FAT
A fat is an essential nutrient which allows your body to store energy. We produce fats naturally, but we also require consuming fats from different foods to gain them.
A well balanced diet full of the right nutrients is key
Too much or too little calories can be bad for you
All of these components help maintain healthy bodily functions
Eating healthier can make you feel better and help fulfill a healthy lifestyle.
